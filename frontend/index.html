Advanced Stats Guide

PER

Player Efficiency Rating - overall player efficiency per 100 possessions (15+ is excellent)

TS%

True Shooting % - shooting efficiency including FGs, 3s, and FTs (>57% is excellent)

AST%

Assist % - % of teammate FGs player assisted while on court

STL%

Steal % - steals per 100 defensive possessions

BLK%

Block % - blocks per 100 opponent 2-pt attempts

USG%

Usage % - % of team possessions used by player (higher for primary scorers)

WS

Win Shares - estimated wins contributed by player

BPM

Box Plus/Minus - +/- per 100 possessions (adjusted for team)
